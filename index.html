  • W. Military Structure Fire (Photo)
    At 04:06 am Sunday morning, 12/31/23, Douglas County Fire District No.2 responded to a reported possible structure fire on West Military Avenue in Roseburg. The first arriving fire crew reported a medium size shop that was fully involved with other structures threatened. A camp trailer parked nearby suffered minor heat damage, the shop and all its contents were a total loss. The cause of the fire is undetermined due to the extent of damage, but it’s believed the fire started from a possible electrical issue from a nearby chicken coop.  DCFD No.2 responded with 3 fire engines, 1 water tender and 1 Command vehicle, Fire Crews were assisted by Lookingglass Fire department, DCSO, Roseburg PD, and PP&L.
